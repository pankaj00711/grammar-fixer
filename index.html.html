Common Mistakes We Fix

Specially trained for Indian, Filipino, and Indonesian English patterns

📌 Articles (a, an, the)

"I went to market" → "I went to the market" — one of the most common ESL mistakes.

📌 Tense Errors

"I am doing it yesterday" → "I did it yesterday" — correct verb tense usage.

📌 Prepositions

"Discuss about the topic" → "Discuss the topic" — removing redundant prepositions.

📌 Subject-Verb Agreement

"He don't know" → "He doesn't know" — fixing third-person verb forms.

📌 Word Order

"My boss he told me" → "My boss told me" — fixing double subjects.

📌 Spelling & Punctuation

Fixes typos, missing commas, capitalization, and other common spelling errors.

💡 ESL Grammar Tips

1Use "the" before specific nouns: "the meeting", "the report".
2Don't double the subject: say "My boss told me", not "My boss he told me".
3Use "discuss" without "about" — "discuss the topic" is correct.
4For past events, use simple past: "I went", not "I am going".
5He/she/it takes -s: "He works", "She runs", "It happens".
